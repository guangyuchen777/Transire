Transire

Transire is a physical tool to ease up the grieving process
by using the grief therapy method - Ritual of Continuity.

It uses the data from the internet and social media
to generate a unique orb that would represent the personality of the deceased one.

About us

This website is a digital visualization/prototype for the grieving tool - Transire. Transire stores a digital representation of the deceased person’s personality to accompany the people who need continuity therapy or those who feel lonely during the mourning process, which aims at helping these people alleviate their sorrow.
